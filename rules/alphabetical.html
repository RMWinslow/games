Lamarckian Poker

YET TO PLAY

(3+) Poker Hands, Simultaneous Play. Standard Deck, no Jokers.

  • Deal 4 cards to each player.
  • Each turn:
    • Flip four cards from the deck into a pool.
    • Each player plays a card from their hand, face down, and reveals simultaneously.
    • In order from highest played card to lowest, each player:
      • Takes all cards from the pool that match the rank or suit of their card.
      • Places their card into the pool.
    • All cards left in the pool are discarded.
  • Turns continue until the deck runs out.
  • Players use the cards in their hand to form a single poker had of 5 cards.

Ocean's Eleven

YET TO PLAY

(2) Poker Hands, Push Your Luck. 2 Standard Decks, no Jokers.

  • Each Player takes a Deck of Cards.
  • Each duel:
    1. Both players start by flipping a card.
    2. Each player forms the best poker-hand they can from their flipped cards.
    3. The player with the worse poker hand chooses one of the following:
      • Flip over another card. The duel then returns to step 2.
      • Concede. The Duel ends and the other player scores a point.
  • First player to 6 points wins..

Fox in the Forest

YET TO PLAY Joshua Buergel

(2) Trick-Taking. 3 Suits, A-10 and Q.

  • Cards are ranked QX98765432A, high to low. (Ace low).
  • Each hand, deal 13 cards to each player. Flip a card to determine Trump.
  • Some cards have special effects when played in a trick:
    • A: You lead the next Trick if you lose this one.
    • 3: Immediately swap the trump card with a card from your hand.
    • 5: Immediately draw one card and then place a card from your hand on the bottom of the deck.
    • 7: +1 point to whoever takes this trick.
    • 9: If only one 9 was played at the end of a trick, treat it like a trump suit.
    • Q: If your opponent can follow suit, they must either play an A or their highest card in this suit.
  • End of Hand Scoring:
    • 0-3 Tricks: +6 Points
    • 4 Tricks: +1 Points
    • 5 Tricks: +2 Points
    • 6 Tricks: +3 Points
    • 7-9 Tricks: +6 Points
    • 10-13 Tricks: +0 Points
  • First player to 16/21/35 points wins.
